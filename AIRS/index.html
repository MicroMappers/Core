AIRS

Artifical Intelligence for Resillience Socities

The Artificial Intelligence for Resilience Socities platforam, or AIRS, is a joint collaboration between the Qatar Computing Research Institute(QCRI) and the Rockefller Foundation's 100 Resilient Cities Initiative. AIRS enables member cities to monitor Twitter traffic in their cities-- Twitter traffic relevant to a city's overall resilience.
Member cities use AIRS to create "machine learning classifier" for indicators of resilience. They then use AIRS to monitor these indicators of resilience in real-time. AIRS is an experimental platform. Additional research is needed to ground-truth these Twitter-based resilience indicators.

GeoTweet Clicker tutorial

Hi! Many thanks for volunteering your time as a digital humanitarian. Disaster responders need to know where damage and needs are. This critical information is often captured on Twitter in real time, which is where you come in.

Being a Digital Humanitarian Volunteer is as easy and fast as a click of the mouse. This app will simply display a tweet and ask you to map it if possible. If the tweet refers to a location (name of city, town, village, etc), then simply copy/paste it into the Search box and click search. Alternatively, you use the mouse to place a marker on the map.

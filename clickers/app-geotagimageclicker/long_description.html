Disaster responders need your help!

Tropical cyclone Yolanda

Super typhoon Yolanda is heading towards the Philippine coast for a Friday morning landfall packing extreme winds and posing serious threat to several areas in the central regions of the country.

This tropical cyclone is expected to have a high humanitarian impact based on the storm strength and the affected population in the past and forecasted path.

The purpose of this app is to quickly identify tweets relevant to disaster responders.

For any questions, see MicroMappers.com or email Justine@standbytaskforce.com
